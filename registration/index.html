Important Notice

Critical information for all participants

Cross-Event Participation IMPORTANT

Non-technical participants who wish to participate in PromptX (Technical Event) must ensure their team has at least one laptop available for the competition.

  • • Each team must have minimum one (1) laptop for technical events
  • • Laptops must have stable internet connectivity
  • • Organizers will not provide computing devices

PromptX: LLM Platform Disclosure FAIR PLAY

  • • The specific Large Language Model (LLM) will be announced only on the day of the event
  • • This ensures fairness and prevents familiarity-based advantages
  • • The selected LLM will be free to use during the event—no paid subscriptions required

Data Privacy & Responsibility IMPORTANT

⚠️ The LLM platform is a third-party web application. Organizers are not responsible for data entered, storage, or processing of submitted prompts.

  • • Do NOT enter personal, confidential, or sensitive information
  • • Use the platform responsibly and review privacy policies if required
  • • Participation is voluntary and at your own discretion

Only a limited number of teams will be permitted to participate across all events. Selection is based on early registration and slot availability.
