PromptX is a technical event designed to explore the true power of human intelligence in the age of Artificial Intelligence. This challenge focuses on crafting high-quality, structured prompts to generate meaningful outputs from a specified Large Language Model (LLM).

Participants must rely solely on their own creativity and logical thinking to design prompts. The use of AI-generated prompts or automated prompt tools is strictly prohibited. The objective is to demonstrate how effective human communication can shape and control AI responses.

With defined rules and evaluation criteria, PromptX aims to highlight clarity, innovation, structure, and the ability to extract the best possible output from an LLM using purely human-crafted instructions.

Guess the Groove is a non-technical event designed to test observation, memory, and quick analytical thinking through cinematic visual challenges. This competition focuses on identifying movies from carefully selected visual clues such as scenes, cropped images, symbolic frames, or partially revealed snapshots.

Participants must rely solely on their film knowledge, attention to detail, and team coordination to determine the correct movie title within the allotted time. The objective is to demonstrate sharp perception, fast reasoning, and collective decision-making under time constraints.

With structured rounds and clearly defined evaluation criteria, Guess the Groove aims to highlight accuracy, speed, teamwork, and the ability to interpret visual storytelling effectively.
