PromptX

The Human Intelligence Challenge

PromptX is a technical event designed to explore the true power of human intelligence in the age of Artificial Intelligence. This challenge focuses on crafting high-quality, structured prompts to generate meaningful outputs from a specified Large Language Model (LLM).

Participants must rely solely on their own creativity and logical thinking to design prompts. The use of AI-generated prompts or automated prompt tools is strictly prohibited. The objective is to demonstrate how effective human communication can shape and control AI responses.

With defined rules and evaluation criteria, PromptX aims to highlight clarity, innovation, structure, and the ability to extract the best possible output from an LLM using purely human-crafted instructions.
